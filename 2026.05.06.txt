



Since the beginning of 2026, strains of the XFG.1.1 lineage have been prevalent in the United States, persisting to the present. Currently, various SARS-CoV-2 variants are dominating distinct regions and nations.

Information regarding the PE.1 lineage will not be elaborated upon further. For the other families, a detailed explanation of their primary sub-variants will follow.
